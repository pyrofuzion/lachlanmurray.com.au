JUNE 2017

Mustard Seed Art Studio & Gallery

Brand + print

Where it all began. My first client. Vicki reached out to me to help take her passion project and grow it into something bigger. She planted the seed and I helped water it. Mustard Seed was founded on the idea of building a space where everyone—no matter the skill level—could explore their creativity. Vicki has built just that: a place where imagination can flourish. Nestled in the Tuggeranong Valley, Mustard Seed offers a variety of art classes and workshops as well as a gallery space which exhibits Vicki’s artistic works. Vicki wished for a brand that communicated her desire to grow anyone into budding artists. Through an inviting colour palette and soft visual elements, Mustard Seed now has a friendly image that is welcoming of all. Thank you, Vicki. Thank you for giving me a chance and for being my first client.
