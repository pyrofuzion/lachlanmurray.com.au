Below is a timeline of some of the highlights from my year-long adventure as a freelance designer. This endeavour enabled me to do what I love while working with many inspiring and passionate individuals. The level of ambition and enthusiasm that was within every single client that I worked for was deeply impressive and I am humbled to have been invited to be part of the foundational moments of their journeys. Enjoy these glimpses into the stories of some very special people.

June 2017

Mustard Seed Art Studio & Gallery

Brand + print

Where it all began. My first client. Vicki reached out to me to help take her passion project and grow it into something bigger. She planted the seed and I helped water it. Mustard Seed was founded on the idea of building a space where everyone—no matter the skill level—could explore their creativity. Vicki has built just that: a place where imagination can flourish. Nestled in the Tuggeranong Valley, Mustard Seed offers a variety of art classes and workshops as well as a gallery space that exhibits Vicki’s artistic works. Vicki wished for a brand that communicated her desire to grow anyone into budding artists. Through an inviting colour palette and soft visual elements, Mustard Seed now has a friendly image that is welcoming of all. Thank you, Vicki. Thank you for giving me a chance and for being my first client.
